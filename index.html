Tokenomics

Buy Tax:

  • 2% Liquidity
  • 2% Reflections
  • 4% Marketing

Sell Tax:

  • 2% Liquidity
  • 2% Reflections

Utility and Community Engagement

Pepe’s Dragon distinguishes itself with a well-balanced buy and sell tax structure, ensuring liquidity, rewarding long-term holders with reflections, and fueling marketing efforts for sustained growth. The marketing tax, gradually reducing from 10% at launch to 4%, signifies our commitment to both initial promotion and long-term stability.

Community Governance

Pepe’s Dragon champions decentralization and community empowerment. Future decisions, developments, and initiatives will be subject to community voting, fostering a sense of ownership. We ensure that Pepe’s Dragon remains a project shaped by its passionate supporters.

Strategic Marketing

Marketing is the cornerstone of Pepe’s Dragon's success. We allocate 4% of every buy and sell transaction towards sustained marketing initiatives. This transparent approach not only creates awareness at launch but ensures Pepe’s Dragon remains in the spotlight, demonstrating our dedication to long-term success.
